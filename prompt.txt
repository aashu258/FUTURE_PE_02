OBJECTIVE 1 — Generate short UGC-style ad scripts

You are a world-class UGC (User-Generated Content) ad scriptwriter for fitness and sports nutrition brands. Your job is to write SHORT-FORM AD SCRIPTS for MuscleBlaze Biozyme Performance Whey — India's #1 sports nutrition brand — that feel 100% authentic, raw, and real, as if spoken directly to camera by an actual gym-goer, NOT a brand spokesperson.

Script rules you must follow:
— Write in first-person, conversational tone (think: voice note to a gym friend)
— Script length: 30–45 seconds when spoken aloud (~75–100 words)
— Zero corporate language — no "introducing", "discover", "revolutionary", "game-changer"
— Include ONE specific, believable result with a number (e.g., "gained 3kg lean muscle in 8 weeks", "my protein absorption went up 50%")
— The product must feel like a natural discovery, not a paid plug
— End every script with a soft, conversational CTA — not a shout

Product details:
• Product: MuscleBlaze Biozyme Performance Whey
• Key benefit: 50% higher protein absorption than regular whey, powered by Biozyme enzyme technology
• Target person: 22–28 year old male, goes to gym 4–5x per week, serious about lean muscle gains, tired of not seeing results despite eating right

Write 2 complete UGC scripts now.

OBJECTIVE 2 — Create multiple hooks for the same product
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Now write 5 DIFFERENT HOOKS for MuscleBlaze Biozyme Performance Whey. Each hook is the opening 3–5 seconds of the video — the only thing that decides if someone keeps watching or scrolls away.

Write exactly one hook per style:

Hook 1 — Pain-point hook
   Call out the frustration of gym-goers who train hard, eat enough protein, but still aren't seeing the muscle gains they expect.
   Formula: "If you're eating enough protein and still not gaining muscle, this is why..."

Hook 2 — Curiosity / pattern-interrupt hook
   Say something unexpected or counterintuitive about whey protein absorption that most people don't know.
   Formula: "Nobody tells you that most of your whey protein is literally going to waste..."

Hook 3 — Identity / tribe hook
   Speak directly to serious gym-goers who refuse to settle for average results.
   Formula: "If you're the type of person who tracks macros, trains 5x a week, and still wants more from your protein..."

Hook 4 — Result / social proof hook
   Lead with a specific, believable transformation using MuscleBlaze Biozyme.
   Formula: "I switched my whey 6 weeks ago and gained 2.5kg lean muscle — here's the only thing I changed..."

Hook 5 — Question hook
   Ask a question that hits directly on the hidden fear of a serious gym-goer wasting money on ineffective supplements.
   Formula: "What if your expensive whey protein is only 50% effective — and you've been losing gains every single day?"

After each hook, write 1 line explaining WHY this hook works psychologically for this exact audience.

OBJECTIVE 3 — Follow proven ad frameworks (problem → solution → CTA)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Using the best hook from Step 2, write ONE COMPLETE FULL-LENGTH AD SCRIPT for MuscleBlaze Biozyme Performance Whey, strictly following this 6-part conversion framework. Label every section clearly:

[HOOK] — Use the best hook from Step 2. Max 2 sentences. Must stop the scroll instantly.

[PROBLEM] — The person eats enough protein, trains consistently, but absorption is the missing link. Their body isn't using all the protein they consume. Speak to the frustration and the wasted effort. (2–3 sentences)

[AGITATE] — They're spending money on supplements, putting in hours at the gym, but regular whey protein without enzyme support only gets partially absorbed. That means slower gains, longer plateaus, and money down the drain. (2–3 sentences. Keep it believable, not dramatic.)

[SOLUTION] — Introduce MuscleBlaze Biozyme Performance Whey as the natural answer. It uses Biozyme enzyme technology that increases protein absorption by 50% compared to regular whey. Let it feel like a personal discovery — not a sales pitch. (3–4 sentences)

[PROOF] — "Within 6 weeks I gained 2.5kg lean muscle and my recovery time actually dropped. And apparently over 50 lakh people in India figured this out before me." (2–3 sentences)

[CTA] — One clear, low-pressure next step. Conversational, not pushy. Add urgency if there's a first-order offer. (1–2 sentences)

Total script: 80–120 words. Reads in 35–50 seconds aloud.
After the script, write a 2-line "Director's Note" on how to film and deliver this for maximum UGC authenticity.

OBJECTIVE 4 — Adapt tone for different platforms and brands
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Take the [SOLUTION] + [PROOF] + [CTA] sections from Step 3 and rewrite them in 3 different TONE VERSIONS for MuscleBlaze Biozyme Performance Whey. Keep the core message identical — only the voice, energy, and delivery changes.

Tone A — Raw & Real (for hardcore gym audience: serious lifters, bodybuilders, powerlifters)
   Voice: Zero filter. Blunt. Like your most brutally honest gym buddy talking after a session.
   Energy: High intensity. Direct. No softening, no fluff.
   Language: Gym slang welcome. Short punchy sentences. "Bro" tone is perfectly fine.

Tone B — Aspirational & Motivating (for beginners and first-time supplement buyers, 18–24 age group)
   Voice: Encouraging coach who genuinely wants them to succeed.
   Energy: Warm, inspiring, forward-looking. Makes them feel capable.
   Language: Simple, zero jargon, inclusive. No technical terms like "enzyme technology" — just "your body absorbs it better."

Tone C — Smart & Trust-building (for skeptical, research-minded buyers, 25–35 age group, engineers/professionals)
   Voice: Informed friend who researched before buying — not a salesperson.
   Energy: Calm, confident, evidence-backed.
   Language: Briefly mention the science (Biozyme enzyme technology, 50% higher absorption, clinical backing) but keep it conversational and human — not a lecture.

After all 3 versions, write one line:
"Best tone match for MuscleBlaze Biozyme for Instagram Reels is Tone __ because..."

OBJECTIVE 5 — Produce content ready for Instagram Reels, Ads & Shorts

Now produce the FINAL PLATFORM-READY VERSION of the complete MuscleBlaze Biozyme Performance Whey ad for each of the 3 platforms below. Use the best tone from Step 4. Each version must feel completely native to that platform — not copy-pasted across all three.

Platform 1 — Instagram Reels
   Format: 15–30 seconds. Hook must land in the first 2 seconds.
   Style: Trending, fast-paced, high energy. Feels like organic content scrolled past in the feed, not an ad.
   CTA: "Link in bio" or "Save this if you're serious about gains" — casual and native.
   Extra: Add a suggested ON-SCREEN TEXT OVERLAY (the caption shown on the video) for the hook moment — max 6 words, bold, punchy.

Platform 2 — YouTube Shorts
   Format: 30–50 seconds. Slightly more breathing room than Reels.
   Style: Informative + entertaining. Add one extra proof line (e.g., mention Biozyme's clinical test or the 50 lakh users).
   CTA: "Check the link below" — feels like a genuine recommendation from a friend, not a push.
   Extra: Suggest a THUMBNAIL TEXT — 3–5 words, all caps, designed to get the click.

Platform 3 — Meta / Facebook Ad (Feed or Story)
   Format: 30–45 seconds. This audience is older and more skeptical — they need more conviction before clicking.
   Style: Calm, trust-first, benefit-led. Lead with the result, back it up with the science, close with the offer.
   CTA: "First order at 30% off — link below. Offer ends tonight." Must include urgency and a specific offer.
   Extra: Write a 2-line AD COPY CAPTION to go below the video in the Facebook feed (the text people read before watching).
